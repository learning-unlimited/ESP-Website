Module Management for {{ program.niceName }}

Welcome to the module management page for {{ program.niceName }}. This page lists the modules that students and teachers will encounter during registration. You can drag individual modules to reorder them and change whether they are required or not. To add or remove modules, you will need to enable/disable them in the program settings.

Please use common sense and make sure to test everything whenever you change any of these settings! Feel free to contact websupport with any questions.
