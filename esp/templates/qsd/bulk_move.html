Move a block of static pages

The URL of a static page comes from two parts: the 'path' and the 'name.' The path is the location in the ESP data tree where the page is anchored. The name is the 'filename' of the page. For example, the following parameters...

  • Path: Q/Web/about
  • Name: oobleck

will translate to the URL /about/oobleck.html.
